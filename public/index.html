Our services

Application Development

We provide end to end development of your Mobile Application on Android or iOS.

UX Design and Longevity

We handle UX Design requirements and provide advice to ensure that your Apps are future proof and ready to be featured on respective App Stores.

Support Contracts

We specialise in providing long term support to your Apps with our competitive support and handover contracts.

Recruitment and Training

We're glad to help you setup and recruit internal teams, and train them to get up to speed with the codebase as well as platform best practices.

Legacy Codebases

We specialise in maintainence as well as reworking of legacy codebases.

Strategy Analysis and Training

We will help you determine long term mobile strategy, focusing on growth and revenue. We also provide training in Android and iOS.
