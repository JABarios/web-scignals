Tengio story

In 2014, Akshay Dashrath and Luigi Agosti founded Tengio. The pair had previously worked together for several years at an established Android consultancy in London – later they contracted jointly on projects. Their dream was to create a company that would set new standards in mobile solutions. They had no shortage of experience to draw on. They’ve worked for clients such as Songkick, JP Morgan, Burberry, eBay (Gumtree), ASOS and Laundrapp. Combining technical expertise with business flair, they’ve built apps that have had more than ten million downloads on Google Play.
